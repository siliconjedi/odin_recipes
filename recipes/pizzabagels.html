Pizza Bagels

Description

A nostalgic snack for cozy times.

Ingredients

  • 6 Lender's frozen bagels
  • 1 jar Pizza sauce
  • 1/2 lb. Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Place bagels in microwave for 30 seconds to soften.
  3. Shred the mozzarella.
  4. Cut the bagels in half horizontally and coat the top of each half with a thin layer of sauce.
  5. Top with a layer of mozzarella.
  6. Place on cookie sheet and bake in oven until cheese is fully melted.
