Lasagna

Description

A wonderful Italian treat for the whole family. Orange cats love it too!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Ground beef
  • 1 jar Ragu Pasta sauce
  • Salt
  • Garlic Powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • 1 lb. Lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb. Mozzarella cheese
  • 12 oz. Ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Brown and drain the ground beef.
  3. Combine pasta sauce, salt, garlic powder, italian seasoning and ground beef into saucepan and heat through.
  4. Boil a large pot of water.
  5. Add noodlkes to water. When tender, drain and let cool.
  6. Place a layer of noodles on the bottom of a deep baking dish.
  7. Top with a layer of sauce, a layer of ricotta, and a layer of mozzarella.
  8. Repeat until last layer of noodles.
  9. Top with sauce and remaining mozzarella.
  10. Cover with aluminum foil and bake in oven until cheese is fully melted.
